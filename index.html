Getting Started

What is D&D? Dungeons and Dragons (or D&D) is a Table Top Role Playing Game that can be played in many ways, shapes, and forms. If you are a veteran or a new player: Welcome! We have several onging campaigns, which are what we call a series of connected sessions, and we are always happy to accept new players and members to the club. You can sit in on a session or maybe you would like to join our writing workshop where we talk about interest writting techniques to make your TTRPG games even more emmersive and fun. We also have a monthly meet and greet(an M&M if you will) where you can mingle and talk to other members of the club outside of a game session setting.

In additon to D&D, we are open to starting any other TTRPGs or even board games! Contact the club to inquire more!
